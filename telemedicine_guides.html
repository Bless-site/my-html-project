How to Use Telemedicine

Telemedicine connects patients with healthcare professionals via video or chat. This allows for convenient consultations from your home.

Steps for a Successful Tele-Consultation

  1. Choose a doctor and available time slot.
  2. Check that your internet and webcam are working properly.
  3. Describe your symptoms in detail and upload any relevant reports.
  4. Attend the consultation on time and follow the doctor's advice.

Always ensure privacy and a quiet environment during your appointment.
